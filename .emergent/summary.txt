<original_problem_statement>
The user wants to create a smartphone application to connect people who cook at home with people interested in their meals who don't have time to cook.

**Product Requirements:**
- The application should be named Mon Voisin Cuisine.
- Users (cooks and customers) should be able to register and log in.
- Cooks can publish meals with photos, descriptions, prices, portions, and pickup times/locations.
- Customers can search for nearby meals, view details, and place orders.
- The platform owner (the user) should receive a commission on each sale. The commission is 10% of the meal price, with a minimum of €0.50, added to the total price for the customer.
- Meals should not be visible after their pickup date and time have passed.
- Meals with zero portions left should not be visible.
- The app should have a Notre philosophie (Our Philosophy) page explaining the spirit of the community.
- Cooks should be able to either take a photo directly with the camera or choose one from their gallery when creating a meal.
- The app should filter meals by distance, showing nearby options. Cooks should be able to specify a location/address.
- The UI for creating a meal, especially for date and time selection, should be user-friendly (e.g., using a calendar).
</original_problem_statement>
